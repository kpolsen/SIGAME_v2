Karen P Olsen

Karen got her PhD from the Dark Cosmology Centre in Copenhagen, Denmark, during which time she specialized in the simulation of emission lines from galaxies, resulting in the creation of SIGAME. She worked three years as Postdoctoral Exploration Fellow at Arizona State University, where she further developed SIGAME into a mature Python module, released for the first time in 2017. While Karen has a strong theoretical background, she also dealt with observations, taking part in e.g. IRAM and NRAO Summer Schools as well as reducing VLA data and writing ALMA proposals.
Fun fact: Karen does aerial acrobatics.
